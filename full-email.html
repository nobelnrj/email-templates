The FULL Library

The FULL Library bookshelf contains over 400+ titles to engage you and cater to your reading needs, effectively. If you’re interested in reading these books, this guide should help you find it.

Important:

  • All the books in the bookshelf are tagged by cubicle ID. When you take a book to read, return the book to the same cubicle in the bookshelf.
  • A misplaced book will cause an ID mismatch making it impossible to find it in the bookshelf. If you don’t remember the cubicle ID, open the BookBuddy app on the iPad and check the Tags section to find out.

Currently, you can only read books in the library and not loan them out. We are studying the feasibility of loaning out books and this may take some time. Until then, you are welcome to use the library area for your reading.

Guide to using the bookshelf

Book Arrangement

  • Each cubicle in the bookshelf has a unique ID as shown in the picture. Every book within that cubicle is tagged with that ID.
  • There is a library iPad next to the bookshelf. To spot a book, open the BookBuddy app in the iPad and tap the Search icon at the bottom. In the book details window, scroll to the Tags section to find the cubicle ID.
  • Please put the book back in the exact cubicle after reading. If in doubt, use the Bookbuddy app to find the cubicle ID.
  • Do not leave books on the table or elsewhere. If you happen to find books outside the shelf, please put them back.
  • Do not fold the cover or pages of a book; instead, use the FULL bookmark that’s inside each book.
